## 1. How to Log In

### How to Access the System
Open your web browser (Chrome, Edge, etc.) and type this address:

```
https://rkc-erp.example.com
```

> 💡 Ask the administrator (Owner) for the exact address!

### Login Steps

![Login Screen](login_page_1775717250215.png)

1. Type your **email address** in the Email field
2. Type your **password** in the Password field
3. Click the **Sign In** button
4. Wait a moment — the Dashboard will appear! 🎉

> ⚠️ **Forgot your password?** → Ask the Owner to reset it for you.

## 4. Students — Managing Children's Information

Click **Students** in the left menu.

### 4-1. Viewing the Student List

![Student List](students_list_1775717407962.png)

- **Summary cards** at the top show total counts (All, Kindergarten, Academy)
- **Student table** below shows all students
- 🔍 Use the **search bar** to find students by name
- Use **filters** to view by program or status

### 4-2. Registering a New Student

1. Click the **➕ Add Student** button (top right)
2. A popup window appears — fill in the information:
   - **Name** (First Name / Last Name)
   - **Program** (Kindergarten / Academy / Both)
   - **Gender**, **Date of Birth**
   - **Allergy** info (if any)
3. Click **Save**!

### 4-3. Viewing Student Details

Click the **⋯ (three dots) button** next to a student's name to open the detail popup:

![Student Detail](student_detail_dialog_1775751261877.png)

What you can see here:
- 📋 **Basic Info** — Name, gender, birthday, enrollment date
- 🏫 **Assigned Class** — Which class they're in
- 🏥 **Health Info** — Allergies, dental checkup status
- 💊 **Health Management** — View history, add new records

### 4-4. Recording Health Checkups

At the bottom of the student detail, there are two buttons:

| Button | What it does |
|--------|-------------|
| 👁️ **View Checkup History** | Shows all past checkup records |
| ➕ **Add Checkup Record** | Enter new checkup results |

**Dental Checkup** (2 times per year):
- Enter the date, result (Good/Needs Treatment), and any notes

**Health Checkup**:
- Enter height (cm), weight (kg), vision (left/right), blood type, and notes

### 4-5. Editing Student Information

1. Click the **✏️ Edit** button in the student detail popup
2. Make your changes
3. Click **💾 Save**!

> ⚠️ The **Delete button** (🗑️) is only visible to the **Owner**, and only for students who have no billing history.

## 6. Billing — Creating Tuition Bills

Click **Billing** in the left menu.

### 6-1. Viewing Bills

![Billing List](billing_page_1775717451744.png)

- Shows each student's monthly bills
- **Status badges** (colored labels) tell you the current status:

| Color | Status | Meaning |
|-------|--------|---------|
| 🟡 Yellow | **DRAFT** | Still being prepared |
| 🔵 Blue | **ISSUED** | Officially sent out |
| 🟠 Orange | **PARTIALLY PAID** | Only part has been paid |
| 🟢 Green | **PAID** | Fully paid! |
| 🔴 Red | **OVERDUE** | Payment deadline has passed |
| ⚫ Gray | **CANCELLED** | This bill was cancelled |

### 6-2. Creating a New Bill

![Create Billing](create_billing_dialog_1775751356808.png)

1. Click **➕ Create Billing**
2. **Select a student** (from dropdown)
3. **Choose the billing month** (e.g., April 2026)
4. **Enter items and amounts**
5. Click **Create**!

### 6-3. Bill Status Flow

```
📝 Draft (DRAFT) — Still preparing
    ↓ [Click "Issue"]
📮 Issued (ISSUED) — Ready to notify parents
    ↓ [Payment received]
💳 Partially Paid (PARTIALLY PAID)
    ↓ [Fully paid]
✅ Paid (PAID) — Done!
```

### 6-4. What You Can Do with a Bill

Click on a bill to see its details:

- **📮 Issue** — Change from Draft to officially Issued
- **💳 Record Payment** — Add a payment when tuition is received
- **❌ Cancel** — Cancel the bill

> ⚠️ **Edit/Delete** is **Owner only** and only when the bill is in **DRAFT** status.

## 10. Expenses — Submitting Expense Requests

Click **Expenses** in the left menu.

![Expenses](expenses_page_1775717464251.png)

### 10-1. Submitting an Expense

1. Click the **➕ Submit Expense** button
2. Fill in the information:
   - **Title** (e.g., "Textbook Purchase", "Room Repair")
   - **Category** (Supplies, Materials, Facilities, etc.)
   - **Amount**
   - **Description** (What was bought, where, and why)
3. Click **Submit**!

### 10-2. Expense Status Flow

```
📝 Draft (DRAFT)
    ↓ [Submit]
📮 Submitted (SUBMITTED) — Waiting for Owner's review
    ↓ 
✅ Approved (APPROVED) — Owner said "OK"!
 or
❌ Rejected (REJECTED) — Needs to be reviewed again
    ↓ [If approved]
💰 Paid (PAID) — Money has been disbursed
```

> 💡 **Teachers/Staff**: Write and submit your expense — that's all you need to do!  
> 💡 **Owner**: Review submitted expenses and choose to approve or reject.

## 14. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### ❓ I can't log in!

> **Things to check:**
> 1. Make sure your email address is typed correctly
> 2. Check for typos in your password
> 3. Make sure Caps Lock is NOT turned on
> 4. If it still doesn't work → **Ask the Owner to reset your password**

### ❓ Some menus are missing!

> Different roles can see different menus.  
> For example, Partner accounts can only see Dashboard, Billing, and Invoices.  
> If you need more menus → **Ask the Owner to change your role**.

### ❓ I don't see a Delete button!

> The Delete function is **only available to the Owner**.  
> This is a safety measure to prevent important data from being accidentally deleted.  
> If something needs to be deleted → **Ask the Owner**.

### ❓ I can't edit a bill!

> Bills can only be edited when they are in **DRAFT** status.  
> Once **ISSUED**, they cannot be modified.  
> In this case → **Cancel** the bill and create a new one.

### ❓ I can't delete a student!

> Students with billing history cannot be deleted.  
> This rule exists to maintain accurate financial records.  
> If a student has graduated or left → **Change their status to "Withdrawn"** instead.

### ❓ I changed my password but can't log in!

> You may be **automatically logged out** after changing your password.  
> Simply log in again with your **new password**!

### ❓ The screen looks weird!

> 1. **Refresh** the browser (press F5 or Ctrl+R)
> 2. Use the latest version of **Chrome** or **Edge**
> 3. Check your internet connection
> 4. If the problem continues → Contact the administrator

### ❓ I'm a partner but want to see student information!

> Partner accounts **cannot access student information**.  
> This is a **privacy protection** policy for the children.  
> If you need specific information → The Owner can share it with you.
